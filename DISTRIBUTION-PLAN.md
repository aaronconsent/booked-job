# Booked Job — Aggressive Distribution Plan (Marketing 101 launch)

**Asset:** 10-lesson Marketing 101 video course + 10 companion pillar articles + ~40 shorts (to build).
**Goal:** max eyeballs → subscribers/followers → email list → done-for-you marketing clients.
**ICP:** owner-operator contractors (plumbing, HVAC, roofing, electrical, tree).

---

## The funnel (everything serves this)
**Shorts (discovery) → Lessons + Articles (authority) → booked-job.com + email (capture) → service.**
Shorts are 90% of new reach. The course/articles are the payoff. The site captures.

- **TIER 1 (native, daily, real effort):** YouTube (Shorts + playlist), Facebook (Reels + Page + *groups*), Instagram Reels, TikTok. This is where contractors are.

- **TIER 3 (autopilot syndication, zero creative time):** Blogger, Tumblr, Telegraph, Bluesky, Mastodon, Threads, Telegram, Pinterest, Medium. Already automated via `content/syndication_queue.json`.
- **HOME BASE:** booked-job.com pillar articles + playlist embed + email capture (the SEO/AEO anchor).

## 30-DAY LAUNCH CALENDAR

### Week 0 — Pre-launch (build anticipation)
- Channel banner ✅, description ✅, links ✅, playlist ✅
- Post 3 teaser shorts: "I made a free 10-part marketing course for contractors."
- Publish 3 pillar articles (Lessons 1, 2, 5) — let Google/AI start indexing
- Set Lesson 1 as channel trailer; feature the playlist on channel home

### Week 1 — LAUNCH
- **Day 1 (binge drop):** flip all 10 lessons + playlist to Public. Big same-day push across Tier 1. Pin CTA comment. Email blast to the list. Personal LinkedIn + X post from Aaron.
- **Days 2–7:** 1 short/day/platform (Lesson 1–2 clips). Publish 1 article/day. Reply to EVERY comment within the hour.
- Drop the course link in 3–5 contractor Facebook groups + relevant subreddits (r/Contractor, r/HVAC, r/Plumbing, r/Electricians) — as value, answering real questions.

### Weeks 2–4 — Sustain
- **Daily:** 1 short/platform (work through all ~40 clips). 1 article/day until all 10 are out (then evergreen reposts).
- **Weekly "featured lesson":** spotlight one lesson + its article + its clips as a themed week.
- **Engagement:** reply to all comments; pin a CTA; DM new followers a "start here" link.
- **Paid (small, surgical):** once a short over-performs organically, boost it $5–10/day. Retarget 50%+ viewers → the course/site. Tiny YouTube in-feed campaign → playlist.
