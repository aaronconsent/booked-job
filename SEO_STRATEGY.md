# Booked Job — SEO / AEO Strategy (research-backed, 2026)

Aggressive, risk-tolerant organic play for a BRAND-NEW domain, complementing the social/Reels
engine. Target = the **contractor-as-reader** (business/peer queries), NOT homeowner service queries.
Soft-funnels to Consent Resolve. Full research in session history (Reddit/BHW/AEO practitioner sources).

## Core insight (2026)
- New domains are **trust-gated**: ~3–9mo sandbox, indexing throttled by trust not volume (one site:
  569 AI posts → 5% indexed). No tactic reliably skips this on a clean domain.
- BUT the **AI-citation door is wide open for underdogs**: only 38% of AI-Overview citations come from
  top-10 (was 76%); on-page GEO levers help low-ranked pages most (+115%). Get cited before ranking.
- Strategy: **depth + original data on-site, borrowed authority off-site (Reddit/YouTube/PR),
  AEO-formatting throughout.**

## Plays (highest leverage first)
1. **Money cluster = "Angi / Thumbtack / HomeAdvisor"** — highest intent, funnels to CR. Own "is Angi
   worth it", "X alternatives", "X vs Y", "Angi Leads cost". Lead with: **Angi CPA ~$1,430–2,500/booked
   job vs ~$290 organic**; leads shared 5–12 pros; Thumbtack 2025 model change cut network rev ~79%.
2. **Reddit = #1 borrowed-authority channel** (Google $60M deal → #2 visibility; ~47% Perplexity / ~49%
   AI-Overview citations). Seed in **business subs (r/sweatystartup, r/smallbusiness, r/Entrepreneur)**
   — NOT trade subs (they dogpile marketers). Warm accounts: 250+ karma / 60 days, comments>posts,
   10:1, no promo before day 14, 24h+ between similar links (shadowban guard).
3. **AEO formatting on every page** (Princeton GEO, zero-cost): ≥3 cited stats, ≥1 quote, real trade
   terms, listicle/comparison-table format, 40–60w direct answer atop each section. Skip llms.txt
   (does nothing for citations). Off-site brand mentions + YouTube beat backlinks 3:1 for AI visibility.
4. **Link/funnel magnets:** "True cost of an Angi lead" calculator (built, in the pillar) + per-trade
   estimate/invoice templates.
5. **Original data = unfair advantage:** collect real contractor quotes/numbers via FB/Reddit prompts +
   /share-your-story/ + fb_engage inbox → turn into digital-PR ("we asked 300 plumbers…") via
   Featured.com / Qwoted. Social channel feeds the SEO weapon.

## Content production (avoid deindex)
Scaled Content Abuse policy (method-agnostic). **Two landmines NEVER touch:** (1) programmatic
"[trade] in [city]" pages w/o unique data (98%-deindex), (2) paid parasite SEO on news sites (manual
penalty). Rule: **human-gate every post + one non-clonable data point ("information gain") per post.**
Start 3–5 posts/week depth-first; scale only as indexing holds. Topical clusters: finish 8–12 posts per
pillar before opening the next.
