# Booked Job — Podcast Strategy

## The show
**Format:** short (6–10 min), high-frequency (start 3×/week → daily), two-host Q&A. Every episode answers ONE question a contractor searches.
**Hosts (ElevenLabs voices):**
- **The Foreman** (Torque Marshal voice — the one from the course) — dead-level, gives the no-BS answer.
- **The Skeptic** — a been-burned veteran who pushes back, calls BS, and asks what the listener is thinking. The friction is the show.
**Why it wins:** the saturated market is all human-hosted guest interviews (can't fake with AI). A daily short search-show is something no human will grind out — and AI makes frequency free. Frequency = search surface = discovery.

### Working names (pick one)
1. **The Tailgate** — two guys talking shop at the back of the truck *(recommended — memorable, on-vibe, searchable)*
2. **Booked: No-BS Marketing for the Trades** — brand-forward, clear
3. **Drive Time** — leans into the truck-listening habit
4. **Cut the Crap** — the brand voice as a title

### Per-episode structure (~8 min)
1. **Cold open (0:00–0:30):** the searched question + a hot take. ("Is Angi worth it? Short answer: usually not — and here's the math that proves it.")
2. **The friction (0:30–6:00):** Skeptic pushes ("everyone I know uses it"), Foreman answers with the real number/story. Back-and-forth, one worked example.
3. **Do this Monday (6:00–7:30):** one concrete action.
4. **Sign-off:** "Get found, get picked, get booked. booked-job.com."

## Discovery playbook (how it ACTUALLY gets found)
A podcast doesn't get discovered as a podcast. It gets discovered the way we already built:
1. **YouTube-first video episodes** — #1 podcast platform (1B+ listeners, beats Spotify/Apple). Audio syndicates out via RSS.
2. **Instant catalog day one:** publish the 10 course lessons as the first 10 audio episodes — real depth, live on all 3 platforms immediately.
3. **Episode titles = exact search queries** (below). Spotify/Apple/YouTube search is the discovery, since they won't recommend a tiny show.
4. **Clips = the vertical shorts we built.** Each episode → 2–3 shorts → the daily social drip → back to the show.
5. **Descriptions sell the play** (the #1 reason people hit play) — auto-generated, keyword-rich.

## Production pipeline (to build)
1. **Script generator** — turn each lesson/article/topic into a two-voice Q&A script (Foreman + Skeptic), ~1,100 words.
2. **Two-voice audio** — ElevenLabs alternating speakers, stitched, mastered to podcast loudness (~−16 LUFS), intro/outro sting.
3. **Video version** — doodle hosts or a clean branded audiogram with synced captions (for YouTube).
4. **Distribution:** YouTube via the API (we have it) · **podcast RSS feed** generated + audio hosted on booked-job.com → one-time submit to Spotify & Apple (they pull from RSS).
5. **Clips** — auto-cut 2–3 shorts per episode into the existing shorts/social machine.

## One-time setup needed from you
- A **Spotify for Podcasters** + **Apple Podcasts Connect** account (free) to claim the show once the RSS feed is live. After that, every new episode auto-distributes from the feed.

## Recommended first move
Build the pipeline + produce **one pilot episode** ("Is Angi Worth It?") — full two-voice audio + the YouTube video version — for you to hear the Foreman/Skeptic dynamic before we batch the catalog.
