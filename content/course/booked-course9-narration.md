# COURSE 9 — GET FOUND BY GOOGLE AND AI
### Booked / booked-jobs.com · Marketing 101 · Course 9
**Voice:** same dead-level foreman. Swearing light, on the punchlines.

---

## THE NARRATION (TTS-ready)

**BEAT 1 — COLD OPEN**

VO: For twenty years, getting found meant one thing — show up on Google when somebody types plumber near me. That's still true. But there's a new front door now, and it's growing fast. People are straight-up asking a chatbot who to hire. So today, we get you found in both places — the search bar, and the robot.

`ON SCREEN: "GET FOUND BY GOOGLE AND AI" — Course 9`

---

**BEAT 2 — SEO IN PLAIN ENGLISH**

VO: First, the old reliable. S-E-O. Forget the jargon — it just means showing up when somebody searches your trade in your town, without paying for the click. It's slow. It compounds. It's basically free. Think of it like a tree — you plant it, nothing happens for a while, and then one day it's shading your whole yard with free leads.

`ON SCREEN: SEO = show up free for "[trade] near me"`

---

**BEAT 3 — THE NEW DOOR: AI**

VO: Now the new one. A-E-O — answer engine optimization. Fancy name, simple idea. About a third of people now start with an A I tool instead of a search bar. They type, who's the best plumber in my area, and the robot just hands them a couple of names. You want to be one of those names. If you're not, you're invisible to a third of your market and you don't even know it.

`ON SCREEN: AEO = get named when they ask the AI`

---

**BEAT 4 — WHO THE AI NAMES**

VO: So how does the robot pick? Same way a smart customer would. It reads the web and looks for the business that shows up everywhere, says clearly what it does, and has the reviews to back it up. The contractor with a strong Google profile, a clear website, and a pile of recent reviews gets named. The ghost with a blank profile does not. The AI isn't magic — it's just repeating what the internet already says about you.

`ON SCREEN: clear pages + reviews + everywhere = the AI names you`

---

**BEAT 5 — ONE FOUNDATION FEEDS BOTH**

VO: And here's the beautiful part — you've already been building this. The exact same stuff that wins on Google wins with the A I. A claimed profile. Real reviews. A clear website that says what you do and where you do it. You don't need two strategies. You need one solid foundation, and it feeds both doors at the same time.

`ON SCREEN: same foundation → ranks on Google AND named by AI`

---

**BEAT 6 — CONTENT THAT ANSWERS QUESTIONS**

VO: One more lever. Answer the questions people actually ask. How much does a water heater cost to replace. Why is my A C freezing up. Put real, plain answers on your site. Google rewards it, the A I quotes it, and the customer trusts you before they ever call. Be the one who answered the question, not the one who hid the price.

`ON SCREEN: answer real questions → Google + AI quote you`

---

**BEAT 7 — DO THIS NOW**

VO: Homework, takes two minutes. Pull out your phone. Google your trade plus your town — see where you actually rank. Then open an A I tool and ask it who the best in your area is. See if your name comes up. Wherever you're not showing up — that's your map. That's exactly where the work is.

`ON SCREEN: Google yourself + ASK the AI — where do you rank?`

---

**BEAT 8 — CLOSE / BRIDGE TO COURSE 10**

VO: So that's getting found, in both worlds. Plant the tree, build the one foundation, answer the questions, and you show up whether they search or they ask. Now — we've covered every door. Last thing left is to put it all on one page so you can run it like an owner instead of a guess. That's your scorecard, Course ten, the finale. See you there.

`ON SCREEN: NEXT → Course 10: Your Marketing Scorecard`
