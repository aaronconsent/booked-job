# COURSE 3 — WHERE CUSTOMERS ACTUALLY FIND YOU
### Booked / booked-jobs.com · Marketing 101 · Course 3
**Runtime target:** ~3:45–4:15 · **Voice:** same dead-level foreman. Swearing light, on the punchlines.

---

## THE NARRATION (TTS-ready — every `VO:` line is the spoken track, in order)

**BEAT 1 — COLD OPEN**

VO: Customers don't find you by magic. They don't stumble onto your truck and feel a connection. They come through doors. And here's the thing nobody tells you — there are only five of them. Five doors, that's it. Once you can see all five, you stop throwing money at random and start running this like a business.

`ON SCREEN: "WHERE CUSTOMERS FIND YOU" — Course 3`

---

**BEAT 2 — THE FIVE DOORS**

VO: So here they are. Door one, your Google profile and the map — that little pack at the top of the search. Door two, your website. Door three, paid ads. Door four, lead networks, the apps like Angi and Thumbtack. And door five, your reviews and word of mouth. Every single customer you will ever book walks through one of those five. Memorize them.

`ON SCREEN: 5 DOORS — Google · Website · Ads · Lead Apps · Reviews`

---

**BEAT 3 — FREE FOUNDATION vs PAID ACCELERATOR**

VO: Now, two of those doors are free. Your Google profile and your reviews don't cost you a dime, and they're the foundation everything else sits on. The other three — ads, lead apps, paid placement — those cost real money. And here's where most guys screw it up. They ignore the free doors, the ones that actually compound over time, and dump their whole budget into the paid ones. That's building your house starting with the roof.

`ON SCREEN: FREE foundation → THEN paid accelerator`

---

**BEAT 4 — THE HEALTHY MIX**

VO: A healthy contractor doesn't live behind one door. Picture a pie. Maybe forty percent of your work comes from paid — ads and lead apps. Thirty percent from Google and your website. Twenty percent from referrals and repeat customers, the gold. And ten percent from everything else. No single door owns you. That's a business that sleeps at night.

`ON SCREEN: 40% paid · 30% Google/site · 20% referral · 10% other`

---

**BEAT 5 — ALL EGGS, ONE BASKET**

VO: Now here's the nightmare. The guy who gets a hundred percent of his work from one app. One door. He's not a business owner — he's a renter. The day that app jacks its prices, or changes the rules, or just shuts his account off with no warning, his phone goes dead silent. I've watched it happen. One door is not a strategy. It's a time bomb.

`ON SCREEN: 100% one app = a TIME BOMB`

---

**BEAT 6 — DIFFERENT TRADE, DIFFERENT DOORS**

VO: And which doors matter most depends on your trade. Emergency work — plumbing, H-V-A-C, a busted anything — that lives and dies on Google and ads, because people search the second it breaks. Bigger project work — roofing, remodels, a new system — that leans harder on reviews and a real website, because folks research before they spend big. Same five doors. Different weight.

`ON SCREEN: emergency → search/ads  ·  big projects → reviews/site`

---

**BEAT 7 — DO THIS NOW**

VO: Here's your homework. Five doors. Circle the ones you actually use right now. Be honest. Most owners circle one, maybe two, and leave the other three wide open for the competition to walk through. The doors you're not using aren't closed — somebody else is standing in them, taking your jobs.

`ON SCREEN: CIRCLE THE DOORS YOU USE`

---

**BEAT 8 — CLOSE / BRIDGE TO COURSE 4**

VO: That's the map. Five doors, a healthy mix, free foundation before paid gas. Now we open them one at a time, and we start with the most valuable free thing you own — the door at the top of the search. That's Course four, your Google Business Profile. It's free, it takes ten minutes, and almost nobody does it right. See you there.

`ON SCREEN: NEXT → Course 4: Your Google Business Profile`

---

## WHAT THEY WALK AWAY KNOWING
1. **There are five doors** customers come through: Google/map, website, paid ads, lead apps, reviews.
2. **Two are free** (Google profile, reviews) and are the foundation — build them before paying.
3. **A healthy mix** spreads across doors; ~40 paid / 30 organic / 20 referral / 10 other.
4. **100% one channel = a time bomb** (you're a renter, not an owner).
5. **Trade changes the weighting** — emergency skews search/ads, big projects skew reviews/site.
