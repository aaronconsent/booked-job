## 🎯 Do these next — biggest reach, no Apple-style headaches

### 1. Amazon Music + Audible + Alexa  ⭐ (huge — gets you on Alexa)
- Go to **https://podcasters.amazon.com**
- Sign in with any Amazon account → **Add your podcast** → paste the RSS feed.
- Reaches Amazon Music, Audible, and "Alexa, play Get Booked Not F***ed."

### 2. iHeartRadio  ⭐
- Go to **https://www.iheart.com/content/submit-your-podcast/**
- Paste the RSS feed + the owner email. Free. Review can take a few days.

### 3. YouTube Music (Podcasts)  ⭐ (you already have the channel)
- **studio.youtube.com** → **Settings → Channel → Advanced**, or the **Podcasts** section → **"Add an existing podcast via RSS."**
- Paste the feed → verify via the hello@aaron.chat code. Your episodes then show as a podcast in YouTube Music.
