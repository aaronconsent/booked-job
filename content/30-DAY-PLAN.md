2. **Days 6–30:** the 16-article money cluster **alternating** with the meme/UGC rotation — every other day is a high-value article or podcast post, so it never reads as spam
3. **Podcast Eps 2 & 3** seeded mid-run; **6 podcast clips** (3 spite-ad commercials + speed-to-lead + 2 Porno-or-Plumbing rounds) roll out as Reels

## Growth levers (the "semi-aggressive" part)
- **Reels are the reach engine** — bumped 2→4/wk; all queued ones are pre-rendered = **0 ElevenLabs credits**
- **Native copy everywhere** — each channel gets distinct, channel-shaped text (no duplicate-content penalty)
- **Engagement, not broadcast** — daemons surface real contractor threads daily for genuine replies (the actual follower driver; no auto-spam = no ban risk)
- **Podcast push** — every podcast post tags `@PlayMorePods #PodcastsOnAmazonMusic` for Amazon Music reshares
- **One asset → many** — each blog article = FB post + 9-channel syndication; each podcast ep = clips + a post + a blog cross-link

## What to watch (Monday strategist report)
- **Followers + video views** by channel (warm-up: small for ~2–3 weeks, that's expected)
- **Which archetype lands first** — course, podcast, cluster, or memes → double down on the winner
- **First real performance signal** vs goals.json; recalibrate targets

## Auto-refill safety net
The weekly strategist (Mondays ~8a) tops up any queue under threshold (FB <5, Reels <3) and writes a fresh article — so the machine won't go dark even past day 30.
