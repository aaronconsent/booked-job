🎬 Video = the engine
Original faceless video on FB Reels + YouTube Shorts. ~50% of the FB feed is now from accounts you don't follow (a new page can go viral), and YouTube is the strongest single AI-citation signal there is. ~5 videos/week across both.
🔍 The "Angi" money cluster
Own "is Angi worth it / alternatives / vs." Real cost ~$1,430–2,500 per booked job vs ~$290 organic. Highest-intent search in the niche — and it funnels straight to Consent Resolve.
🤖 Built to get AI-cited
Only 38% of AI-Overview citations now come from top-10 results, and the on-page levers help underdog pages most. Every page: cited stats, quotes, comparison tables, 40–60w direct answers.
💸 $7/day retargeting
Cheap broad video-views build a warm pool, then retarget the watchers into follows — far better than cold page-likes.
👷 Own the audience → CR
FB group + email + owned site. Build channels that keep producing after you stop paying, then soft-funnel the warm audience to Consent Resolve — the "owned leads, never resold" play.
📊 Original data = PR weapon
Collect real contractor numbers (FB, Reddit, /share-your-story) → turn into digital PR. Brand mentions beat backlinks 3:1 for AI visibility.
