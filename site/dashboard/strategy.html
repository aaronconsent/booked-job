🧭 Strategy

🎬 Video = the engine
Original faceless video on FB Reels + YouTube Shorts — now 4 Reels/week, plus vertical clips cut from the podcast. ~50% of the FB feed is from accounts you don't follow (a new page can go viral), and YouTube is the strongest single AI-citation signal there is.
🎙️ The podcast
"Get Booked, Not F***ed" — a raw, R-rated marketing show for the trades. Live on Spotify via a self-hosted RSS feed (Apple + indie apps next). Every episode becomes vertical clips that feed Reels and a blog article that feeds SEO. One recording → many assets.
🔍 The 16-article money cluster
Own "is Angi worth it / cost per booked job / pricing / reviews / speed to lead." 16 pillar articles, each with cited stats and a 40–60w direct answer. Real cost ~$1,430–2,500 per booked job vs ~$290 organic — highest-intent search in the niche, funnels straight to Consent Resolve.
📡 13 channels, native copy
Every article fans out to 13 connected channels — FB, IG, YouTube, Blogger, Tumblr, Telegraph, GitHub Pages, Bluesky, Mastodon, Threads, Telegram, Pinterest, LinkedIn — each with unique, channel-shaped copy. Facebook posts 7 days/week; links in the first comment only.
🤖 Built to get AI-cited
Only 38% of AI-Overview citations come from top-10 results, and on-page levers help underdog pages most. Every page: cited stats, quotes, comparison tables, 40–60w direct answers.
👷 Own the audience → CR
FB group + email + owned site + podcast subscribers. Build channels that keep producing after you stop paying, then soft-funnel the warm audience to Consent Resolve — the "owned leads, never resold" play.
💸 $7/day retargeting + original data
Cheap broad video-views build a warm pool to retarget into follows. And real contractor numbers (FB, Reddit, /share-your-story) become original-data PR — brand mentions beat backlinks 3:1 for AI visibility.
