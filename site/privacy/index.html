Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Booked Job ("we," "us") publishes content and tools for home-service business owners. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit booked-job.com or interact with our content, and how we use it. We keep this short and plain, because that's how we'd want it.

What we collect

  • Email address — only if you choose to subscribe to our newsletter. We ask for nothing else.
  • Basic usage data — standard, aggregated analytics (pages visited, general location, device type) to understand what content is useful. This does not identify you personally.
  • We do not collect financial information, and we do not sell your data to anyone. Ever.

How we use it

  • To send you the newsletter you signed up for.
  • To understand which content helps our audience so we can make more of it.
  • To operate and secure the website.

Service providers we use

We rely on a few trusted third parties to run the site. They only process data as needed to provide their service:

  • Resend — delivers our email newsletter and stores subscriber email addresses.
  • Cloudflare — hosts and protects the website.
  • Social platforms (Facebook, YouTube, Instagram, Pinterest, Bluesky, Mastodon, and others) — when we publish content to them, their own privacy policies govern any data they collect from you there.

Cookies

We use minimal, essential cookies needed for the site to function and for basic aggregated analytics. We do not use cookies to build advertising profiles of you.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe anytime using the link in any newsletter email.
  • Request deletion of your email from our list by contacting us (below).
  • You can browse the site without subscribing or providing any personal information.

Children

Booked Job is for business owners and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll revise the "last updated" date above. Material changes will be noted on this page.

Contact

Questions about your privacy or this policy? Email hello@booked-job.com.
